Home Details for 174 W 76th St #8G

9 Days on Reland 302 views

You can HAVE IT ALL in this beautifully renovated 2 bedroom pre-war home in one of the few Rosario Candela buildings on the Upper West Side. The home offers a gracious layout with spacious rooms, a renovated kitchen, and a luxurious marble bath. Some of its many other features include open north city views from the living room and master bedroom and a window seat to enjoy them from, high ceilings with crisp crown moldings, parquet floors, custom made built-ins, a washer / dryer, plenty of storage, and brilliant light throughout. The Wellston (AKA - 161 West 75th Street) offers full-time ... doormen, a live-in superintendent, bicycle room, storage bins (for a fee), and an outdoor patio. All this in one of the city's most sought after locations - convenient to the express subway, Central Park, Riverside Park, The Museum of Natural History, shopping, and delicious restaurants. Do not miss this opportunity. Call today to schedule your appointment.
